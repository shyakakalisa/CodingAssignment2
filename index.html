Kenyan Kuku (Spiced Chicken)

Recipe by: Shyaka Kalisa

Ingredients

  • 1 whole chicken, cut into pieces
  • 2 onions, chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 tomatoes, diced
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooking oil

Equipment

  • Large cooking pot
  • Knife
  • Cutting board
  • Wooden spoon

Directions

  1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat.
  2. Add onions and garlic, sauté until fragrant.
  3. Add chicken pieces and brown lightly.
  4. Add tomatoes and spices, then stir.
  5. Cover and simmer until chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Season to taste and serve warm.
